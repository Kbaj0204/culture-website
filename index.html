About India


India, officially the Republic of India, is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area, the second-most populous country, and the most populous democracy in the world

Its government is a constitutional republic that represents a highly diverse population consisting of thousands of ethnic groups and likely hundreds of languages. With roughly one-sixth of the world’s total population, India is the second most populous country, after China

India is known for many, many different aspects – its food, culture, its massive population, its natural landscapes, its languages, classical dances, Bollywood or the Hindi film industry (famous Indian figures such as Amitabh Bachchan, Shah Rukh Khan, Aishwarya Rai), the birthplace of yoga, spirituality, natural beauty, its warm, welcoming citizens… the list can go on.

There you have it. We hope you learned some fun facts about India! Have you visited India before? What did you think of it?
